MY TOP 5 FAVOURITE MOVIES


1. MUJHSE DOSTI KAROGE

The theme of "Mujhse Dosti Karoge" revolves around friendship, love, and sacrifice. It explores the complexities of relationships and the importance of communication in understanding one's feelings. The story highlights the challenges of unrequited love and the power of perseverance in pursuit of happiness. It delves into the idea of destiny and how it intertwines the lives of the characters. Ultimately, the film emphasizes the value of true friendship and the transformative journey it can lead to.

2. FIGHTER

Hrithik Roshan, Deepika Padukone, and Anil Kapoor star in 'Fighter', a high-voltage action adventure film inspired by the real-life Balakot airstrike. The movie pays tribute to the Indian armed forces and specifically focuses on Squadron Leader Shamsher Pathania, whose aviator call sign is 'Patty'.

3. JAWAN

Jawan shares the story of an Indian individual who endured the harshest torture at the hands of a gang that brutally tormented many pitiful regular people while unable to understand the difference between even a little child. He made the decision not to leave anyone to cause harm to society after escaping from the gang. The remaining plot of this movie concerns how he locates the major antagonist and how he aims to take him out.
